# Riches from Below

Riches from Below is a Fabric mod for Minecraft 1.21 that turns treasure-hunting into a game of risk, luck, and clever choices. It introduces a custom currency and a set of playful machines and items that reward bold plays, while keeping enough mystery for you to discover the best outcomes on your own.

Expect new ways to gamble, store, and transform your riches, plus a handful of unique curios that bend the rules in surprising ways. Whether you are chasing jackpots, experimenting with odd artifacts, or just collecting everything shiny, the mod is built to make every pull feel like a story.

Jump in, follow the clues, and let the surprises reveal themselves.
